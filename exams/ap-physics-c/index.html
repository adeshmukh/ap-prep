AP Physics C

Two separate calculus-based exams — Mechanics and Electricity & Magnetism — covered together as thirteen sequentially numbered units under the 2024 College Board CED (effective 2024–25 school year). Disambiguate a unit by subject or by its unique unit number.

13 units total Mechanics 1–7 · E&M 8–13 Separate testing sessions

Mechanics · Units 1–7

Unit Title Exam weighting
1 Kinematics 10–15%
2 Force and Translational Dynamics 20–25%
3 Work, Energy, and Power 15–25%
4 Linear Momentum 10–20%
5 Torque and Rotational Dynamics 10–15%
6 Energy and Momentum of Rotating Systems 10–15%

Note on gravitation. No longer a standalone unit. Gravitational force appears under Unit 2; orbital motion appears under Unit 6 (Motion of Orbiting Satellites).

Exam format · per subject

  • Multiple Choice. 40 questions, 4 options each (A–D), 80 minutes, 50% of score. Administered digitally.
  • Free Response. 4 multi-part problems, 100 minutes, 50% of score. Handwritten.
  • Calculator & reference. Calculator permitted throughout. Official equation and constants reference sheet provided.
  • Scheduling. Mechanics and E&M are administered in separate testing sessions.

Math expectations

Fluency with derivatives, integrals, vector operations, and differential equations of the form used in RC / LR / LC circuits and simple harmonic motion.
